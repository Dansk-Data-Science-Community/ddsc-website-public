About the Award

The DDSC Open Source Award celebrates innovation, collaboration, and excellence in the open source community. This initiative brings together three leading organizations:

  • DDSC (Danish Data Science Community) - Strengthening Denmark's open source culture through community-driven projects
  • IDA - Representing 175,000 engineers, science graduates, and IT specialists who are key to Denmark's digital future
  • DDSA (Danish Data Science Academy) - Advancing data science education and professional development
